<p>We encourage our students to be active participants in their learning.
Whether in the classroom or through outdoor and experiential learning,
our TGStars are given opportunities to put their strengths into action,
learn with and from their peers, and take increasing ownership of their
learning journey.</p>
<p>We know that we cannot do this alone. Supporting our students as <strong>Leaders of Tomorrow, Today</strong> is
a partnership between school and home. We warmly invite parents and caregivers
to work closely with us, to share a common language of strengths and values,
