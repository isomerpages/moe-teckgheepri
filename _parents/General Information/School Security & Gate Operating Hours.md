<h4><strong>School Safety &amp; Emergency Information</strong></h4>
<p>In TGPS, the safety and well-being of every child is our top priority.
We work closely with parents and the wider community to ensure a safe,
orderly, and caring environment for all. Thank you for partnering us in
keeping our students safe.</p>
<hr>
<h4><strong>School Security</strong></h4>
<p>To maintain a safe school environment, all visitors, including parents,
are required to register at the security post when entering the school
for official purposes such as:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Attending meetings</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Fetching an unwell child</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Purchasing books or uniforms (after 2 pm during school days)</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>For safety reasons, visitors should only access areas that have been approved
and should not enter other parts of the school.</p>
